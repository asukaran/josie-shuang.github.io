Novel Project: The Seven Stars Palace

I am writing a Chinese detective novel in the background of the last decade before the collaspe of Ming Dynasty in ancient China (~1630s). This is not only a story replete with crimes, schemes, and confrontations, but is more a portrait of the struggles, distress, and faiths of the lower-class officials and civilians in the turbulent and chaotic world at war in late Ming.

The main characters:

  • Su (the main character): A lower-class official in the Imperial guards.
  • You: Su's assistant, also a lower-cass official in the Imperial guards.
  • Miss Xu: A young daughter of a previleged officer. A hostage of the seven thieves.
  • Cloud: A mysterious killer.
  • The seven thieves: The remnant soldiers after the death of the rebel leader Gao Yingxiang.
Still working on it...
