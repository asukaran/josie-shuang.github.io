Novel Project: The Seven Stars Palace

I am writing a book of a detective story occurring during the last years before the collaspe of Ming Dynasty in ancient China. This story is not only about schemes, deceits, and confrontations, but is more of a portray of the civilians and lower-class officials and their struggles, conflicts, and faiths in the turbulent world in late Ming.
The main characters:

  • Su the Southern Star (the main character): A lower-class official in the Imperial guards.
  • You Demand on the Wind: Su's assistant.
  • Xu the Grace: The 5th daughter of a previleged officer. A hostage of the seven thieves.
  • Catch the clouds: A mysterious assassin.
  • The seven thieves: The remnant soldiers after the death of the rebel leader Gao Yingxiang.
Still working on it...
