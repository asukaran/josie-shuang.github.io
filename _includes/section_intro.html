About Me

I am a second-year PhD student in Clinical Psychology at The Penn State University, State College. I am originally from China. Research-wise, I am currently working with Dr. Stephen Wilson on a project that examines the influences of contextual information on restrained eaters' neural reactivity to food and non-food reward cues. Our goal is to identify the neural markers that predict the motivational processes and food choices among individuals in obesogenic environment.

In my spare time, I enjoy running, reading, and writing my novel!
